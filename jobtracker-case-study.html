01: Problem & Goals

Job Hunting Is
Chaos by Default

Job searching is one of the most high-stakes, emotionally draining activities a person can go through, yet the tools most people use are a patchwork of Google Sheets, browser bookmarks, and frantic calendar reminders. There's no single space that holds it all together.

Existing job tracker apps either feel like stripped-down CRMs or glorified to-do lists. None of them understand the emotional arc of a job search or help users make smarter decisions about where to invest their time. Jobbrief was designed to fix that.

73%
Use Spreadsheets
Most active job seekers track applications in Google Sheets or Excel, tools never designed for this purpose.
60%
Miss Follow-Ups
Over half of job seekers forget to follow up after interviews, often the difference between an offer and silence.
3.2×
Apps Per Search
The average active job seeker juggles 3+ tools simultaneously: email, job boards, notes apps, and calendars.

Side of Job Hunting

Finding 01
Context Collapse
Users apply to 20+ roles but can't remember the details of each company by the time an interview comes around. Notes are everywhere and nowhere.
Finding 02
Follow-Up Paralysis
Even when users intended to follow up, they didn't know when, what to say, or if it would seem desperate. No guidance, no nudges.
Finding 03
Energy Misallocation
Users spent equal energy on every application regardless of signal. Hot leads got the same attention as cold rejections.
Finding 04
Emotional Blindness
No existing tool acknowledged the emotional toll of the process. Users felt like they were failing, even when they were doing well statistically.

"I had a spreadsheet with 40 rows and I genuinely couldn't tell you which ones were worth my time anymore. I just felt paralyzed."

— Interview participant, 27, UX designer actively job searching

⚡ Key Feature: Platform Integration

One of Jobbrief's core differentiators is that you never have to manually enter a job posting. Users connect their existing job search accounts and Jobbrief pulls everything in automatically, keeping your pipeline in sync across every platform you already use.

💼LinkedIn
🔍Indeed

🤝Handshake
📋Glassdoor
💻Dice
🏢ZipRecruiter
📎+ Any Job Board
🧠
How the AI Score Works
Two data sources. One clear number.
📁 Your Data
  • Resume & cover letter you upload
  • LinkedIn profile (if connected)
  • Application history & response rates
  • Interview notes & follow-up records
  • Job preferences you set in onboarding
📡 Market Signals
  • Demand for the specific role & title
  • Salary benchmarks for your location
  • Company's current hiring velocity
  • Competition level in your market
  • Industry hiring trends & seasonal patterns
The score updates in real time as new signals come in: if a company starts interviewing more candidates or a role goes cold, your score reflects it immediately.
01
Smart Dashboard
The home screen surfaces only what matters today: AI-ranked priority applications, follow-up nudges, and a quick pipeline summary. No scrolling through 40 rows.
02
AI Priority Score
Each application receives a 0–100 score based on two inputs: your own data (resume, cover letter, linked profiles, and application history you upload or connect) and live market signals (demand for the specific role, salary benchmarks, company hiring velocity, and competition level in your area). The AI explains its reasoning in plain language, not a black box.
03
Follow-Up Nudges
Contextual reminders that tell you when and why to follow up, drafted and ready to send in one tap. No more guessing or anxiety about reaching out.
04
Pipeline Kanban
A visual board that moves with you: Applied, Screening, Interview, Offer. Cards show AI scores and time-sensitive flags, so the most urgent items are always visible.

05: Results & Impact

Tested With Real
Job Seekers

Usability testing was conducted with 12 participants, all actively job searching at the time. Sessions used a high-fidelity Figma prototype and focused on task completion, comprehension of the AI score, and overall desirability.

91%
Task Completion
Participants successfully identified their top priority application and triggered a follow-up in under 3 minutes.
88%
AI Score Trust
When shown the AI reasoning panel, 88% said they trusted the priority score enough to act on it immediately.
−64%
Anxiety Reduction
Self-reported stress about "not knowing what to do next" dropped by 64% after a single session with the prototype.
9.4/10
Desirability Score
"Would you use this app in your current job search?" 9.4 average. Every participant said yes.
